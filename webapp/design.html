iPad Landscape · Design Preview

Aimee's Feeding Robot

The risk: Dark navy for a medical app. Justified — mealtimes are often in dim settings where bright screens glare; amber on navy exceeds WCAG AAA contrast; the palette reads as warm and premium rather than clinical. Georgia headings add dignity. Landscape layout uses 2-column splits to give each element maximum touch surface.
